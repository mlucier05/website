Tic Tac Toe Decide who will go first. The player who goes first is "x", while the other is "o".
The player who goes first needs to pick a space. They will then change that space to their symbol, either "x" or "o".
Alternate picking spaces until one player has 3 of their symbol in a row (in which case they win), or until no tiles are left to pick (tie).
